kada prasad


Kada prasad is a sacred sweet offered to all visitors at a Gurdwara, the Sikh place of worship. This rich, wheat-based pudding, also known as atta ka halwa, is deeply symbolic and is distributed equally among the congregation, embodying the core Sikh principles of equality, devotion, and selfless service .Kada Prasad or Atte ka Halwa is a rich dessert prepared in Desi Ghee (clarified butter) and whole wheat flour offered to all who visit & pray in a Gurudwara today on the occasion of Gurupurab.
